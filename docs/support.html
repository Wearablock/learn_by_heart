Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new card?
Tap the "+" button on the Cards tab to create a new flashcard. Enter the text you want to memorize and optionally add a hint to help you remember.
How does speech recognition work?
When studying, tap the microphone button to speak your answer. The app uses your device's built-in speech recognition to convert your speech to text and compare it with the correct answer.
What is the pass threshold setting?
The pass threshold determines how similar your answer needs to be to the correct answer. A setting of 80% means your answer needs to match at least 80% to be marked correct.
How does spaced repetition work?
Cards you answer correctly will be scheduled for review at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 7 days, etc.). Cards you get wrong will appear again sooner for more practice.
Is my data backed up?
All data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, your data will be lost. We recommend regularly exporting your cards if available.
Why isn't speech recognition working?
Make sure you've granted microphone permission to the app. Also check that you have an internet connection, as some devices require it for speech recognition.
